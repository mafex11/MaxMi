## Must-fix

1. **Page source IDs are not persisted or passed to completion, so Store-side provenance validation cannot work.**

   `completeAgentRun(runID:ops:)` promises to validate `source_refs ⊆ the run’s page`, but `agent_runs` does not store the page’s source IDs, and the completion API receives neither `sourceIDs` nor a cursor-from tuple sufficient to reconstruct the exact page.

   `StoreAgentRepository` also cannot validate them at completion: it receives only `runID` and DTO ops after `HourlyAgent` has consumed the leased page.

   Fix by persisting `page_source_ids_json` in `agent_runs` at claim time, then validating against it inside `completeAgentRun`. This is simpler and stronger than reconstructing the page. It also makes the provenance validation durable across process restart.

   `renewAgentRunLease` exists on `Store`, but `AgentRepository` exposes no renewal method, so `HourlyAgent` cannot renew while awaiting Gemini.

   ```swift
   func renew(runID: String) async
   func runIfDue() async -> AgentRunOutcome // noWork, completed, failed
   ```
